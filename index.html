Welcome to Migrant Workers!!!


Migrant worker portal is the central database of migrant workers monitored by the Government of India
and administered by the Local State governments. migrant workers are requested to enroll themselves as a
migrant and get a Migrant Worker Security Number(MWSN) which is a 16 digit number that helps the govenments
keep track of the migrants and protect their entitlements by providing them mutual benifits that helps them to
sustain and work in a foriegn state. Migrants are provided with details about the hiring by private companies after
they get the MWSN number.Any migrant can apply for MWSN by vsiting the locally available Worker Privilege Centre(WPC's)
by providing a primary proof confirming their identity.This helps the migrants to have a platform where their progress will
tracked by the local government and any private agencies requiring manpower can also request for Man power from WPC's.

WHERE SHOULD I ENROLL?

WORKERS ARE REQUESTED TO ENROLL THEMSELVES IN THE LOCALLY AVAILABLE WORKER PRIVELEGE CENTERS(WPC'S) AND get A MIGRANT SECURITY NUMBER(MSN).

HOW SHOULD I ENROLL?WHATS MIGRANT WORKER SYSTEM?

UPON YOUR ARRIVAL IN WPC'S MIGRANT WORKERS ARE REQUESTED TO PRESENT
A PRIMARY PROOF WHICH WILL BE USED BY THE LOCAL STATE AUTHORITIES IN IDENTIFYING THE PERSON AND HIS STATE. UPON
SUBBMISSION OF PROOF THEY WILL BE ASKED TO FILL A MIGRANT WORKER SECURITY FORM
FORM.THE DETAILS PROVIDED IN THE FORM WILL HELPS THE LOCAL STATE AUTHORITIES TO DETERMINE THE STATUS OF THE MIGRANT AND DETAILS WILL BE STORED IN MIGRANT WORKER PORTAL.A MIGRANT SECURITY NUMBER WILL THEN BE ISSUED TO THE MIGRANT WORKER AFTER PROPER ASSESMENT.
MIGRANT WORKER SECURITY NUMBER(MWSN) HELPS LOCAL STATE AUTHORITIES TO KEEP TRACK AND IN PROVIDING MIGRANT WITH ENTILEMENTS.
